Iskender Kebab Recipe

Iskender Kebab is a traditional Turkish dish made with thinly sliced lamb or beef, served over pieces of pita bread, and topped with a rich tomato sauce and melted butter. It's often accompanied by yogurt and grilled vegetables.

Ingredients

For the doner kebab:

  • 500gr. tenderloin meat
  • 500gr. ground meat

For the meat marinade:

  • 1 medium onion
  • 2 tbsp yogurt
  • 1 tsp black pepper, ground red pepper
  • 1 tsp oregano (optional)
  • about 4-5 tbsp olive oil

For the ground beef marinade:

  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 medium onion

For the tomato sauce:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp each butter and tomato paste
  • 2 grated tomatoes
  • Salt, black pepper and paprika to taste
  • Enough hot water to make a thick sauce

For serving:

  • Pita bread or rice
  • Yogurt
  • Grilled vegetables (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Meat: Slice the cooked doner-style beef or lamb into thin strips. If using pre-cooked doner, gently reheat in a pan or oven until warm and slightly crispy on the edges.
  2. Make the Tomato Sauce: In a saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add butter and tomato paste, stirring until melted. Add grated tomatoes, salt, black pepper, paprika, and enough hot water to achieve a thick sauce consistency. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Prepare the Yogurt: In a bowl, mix yogurt with a pinch of salt and a drizzle of olive oil. Set aside.
  4. Toast the Bread: Lightly toast pita bread or rice in a pan until warm and slightly crispy.
  5. Assemble the Dish: Place the toasted bread or rice on a serving plate. Top with sliced meat, then pour the tomato sauce over it.
  6. Add Butter & Yogurt: Drizzle melted butter over the dish and add a dollop of prepared yogurt on the side.
  7. Optional Garnish: Add grilled peppers and tomatoes on the side for a traditional touch.
